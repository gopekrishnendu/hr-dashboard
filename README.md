# hr-dashboard
HR DASHBOARD
HR today isn’t about digging through endless spreadsheets.
 It’s about having the right insights, at the right time, to make better people decisions.
This HR Dashboard keeps everything simple and visible in one place:
 • Who’s in the organization and where
 • Attendance and leave patterns
 • Attrition vs retention trends
 • Employee performance
 • Hiring and recruitment progress
When HR data is clear and easy to understand, it becomes powerful.
 Leaders can identify gaps early, support employees better, and move faster with confidence not guesswork.
Data-driven HR isn’t a “nice to have” anymore.
 It’s how modern teams grow, engage, and retain talent.
